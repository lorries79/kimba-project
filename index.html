So, about me?

I'm literally the cutest dog ever.... No, really.

When I was a puppy I never thought I would have a family like mine. It took alot of patience because I liked to eat all of my beds then pretend that they exploded. Now I'm the best girl and love to have belly scrathes in the morning but when it's hot at night I like to sleep under the bed, it's cooler under there.

Even though I hate the car I love the beach and the outdoors. I love water and to play with other dogs (sometimes I get too excited though). I help to pick up things that fall on the floor because I know if I do I will get a treat. I talk alot when I play and like to bark from the balcony at other dogs passing by.

About Me

Hi, my name is Kimba. I was born in 2018 and was adopted from Nuevavida Adopciones by Javi and Grace when I was 4 months old. I had a hard start to my life as I was abandoned with my sister and taken to the shelter where I met my forever family.
